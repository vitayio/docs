title: "Branding and white label"
description: "Customize your logo, colors, and sender identity so candidate-facing pages and emails reflect your firm's brand."
---

Vitay lets you apply your firm's visual identity to the emails and pages your candidates and references see. You can upload a logo, set brand colors, and — on eligible plans — enable full white-label mode with a custom domain.

<Note>
  You need a **root recruiter** (admin) role to change branding settings. Most branding changes take effect immediately for all new outgoing emails and candidate-facing pages.
</Note>

## Upload your logo

Your logo appears on candidate reference reports and on the emails Vitay sends to references.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open General settings">
    Go to **Settings → General**.

    ![Settings → General, with company name and logo](/images/branding-general.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo">
    In the **Logo** section, click **Upload here** and select an image file from your computer. PNG or SVG files with a transparent background work best.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The logo is saved automatically after uploading. Refresh the page to confirm it appears correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set your brand colors

Primary and secondary colors are used on buttons, tabs, and UI elements in candidate-facing and reference-facing pages.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open General settings">
    Go to **Settings → General**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the primary color">
    In the **Primary color** field, enter your brand color as a hex code (for example, `#1CBAC8`) or use the color picker. This color is used for buttons and key interactive elements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the secondary color">
    In the **Secondary color** field, enter your secondary brand color. This is used for supporting UI elements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**.

    ![SCREENSHOT: The color picker fields for primary and secondary brand colors — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enable white-label mode

White-label mode replaces all Vitay branding with your own logo and colors across candidate- and reference-facing pages, and allows you to use a custom domain name.

<Note>
  White-label mode is a paid add-on feature. If you don't see it enabled in your account, contact **support@vitay.io** to enquire about upgrading.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the White Label tab">
    Go to **Settings → White Label**.

    ![The White Label settings tab](/images/white-label.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable white label">
    Turn on the **Enable / Disable white label** toggle. Vitay will immediately start using your logo and colors instead of its own across all candidate-facing surfaces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a custom domain (optional)">
    In the **Domain name** field, enter the domain you want to use (for example, `references.yourcompany.com`). This requires a DNS CNAME record pointing to Vitay — contact **support@vitay.io** for the exact record to add.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add custom links for your team's welcome email">
    Optionally update the **Explainer video link** and **User guide link** fields. New recruiters receive these links in their welcome email, so you can point them to your own onboarding materials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a font (optional)">
    In the **Font family** field, select the typeface used across the application. The default is Open Sans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Email sender branding

Your logo and colors also flow into outgoing emails. To control the sender name and email address references see (separate from visual branding), see [Email sender settings](/account-configuration/email-sender-settings).

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My logo isn't appearing on reports or emails">
    Make sure you have uploaded the logo in **Settings → General** and that white-label mode is enabled (if required by your plan). Also check that the image file is not corrupted — try re-uploading a fresh copy. PNG files under 2 MB with a transparent background are most reliable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Brand colors are not showing on candidate pages">
    Colors are applied to candidate-facing pages when white-label mode is active. If you see default Vitay colors, confirm that the **Enable / Disable white label** toggle in **Settings → White Label** is turned on.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I don't see a White Label tab">
    White-label mode requires a specific plan upgrade. If the tab is missing, your account does not have the feature enabled. Contact **support@vitay.io** to discuss enabling it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My custom domain isn't working">
    Custom domains need a CNAME record in your DNS provider pointing to Vitay's servers. DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate. If it has been longer than that, contact **support@vitay.io** with your domain name so the team can verify the configuration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
