---
title: "Manage users"
description: "Add, remove, and change roles for the recruiters in your Vitay account, including sub-account access."
---

Users are the recruiters and admins who log in to Vitay to manage candidates and references. As an admin (root) user you can invite colleagues, update their roles, and remove access when someone leaves.

<Note>
  You need a **root recruiter** (admin) role to add, remove, or change the roles of other users. If you are a supervisor you can view your team but cannot make structural changes.
</Note>

## Add a user

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Users section">
    From the left-hand navigation, go to **Settings** then select the **Users** tab (sometimes labelled **Team**).

    ![SCREENSHOT: The Settings → Users page showing the team list and an "Add a user" button — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add a user">
    Click the **+ Add a user** (or **Add user**) button in the top-right corner. A modal dialog opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the user's details">
    Fill in the new user's **first name**, **last name**, **email address**, and optionally their **phone number**. The email address becomes their login username and must be unique across Vitay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the role">
    Choose whether this person is a **Recruiter** or a **Root recruiter** (admin). See [Roles explained](#roles-explained) below for the difference. You can also tick **Supervisor** to grant read-only oversight of the whole account without admin privileges.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit**. Vitay creates the account and emails the new user their login credentials immediately.

    ![SCREENSHOT: The Add a user modal with name, email, and role fields filled in — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Roles explained

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **Recruiter** | Add candidates, send reference requests, read their own candidates' reports. |
| **Supervisor recruiter** | Everything a recruiter can do, plus visibility into all team activity and metrics, and the ability to edit question sets. |
| **Root recruiter (admin)** | Full access: all recruiter capabilities plus account settings, user management, billing, and sub-accounts. |

<Note>
  Each Vitay account has exactly one **default root** account that cannot be deactivated or have its role changed. If you need to hand over admin duties, contact Vitay support.
</Note>

## Edit a user's details or role

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the user">
    Go to **Settings → Users** and locate the person in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the edit modal">
    Click the **edit** icon (pencil) next to their name. The same modal used when adding a user opens, pre-populated with their current details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update and save">
    Change the fields you need — name, phone number, or role — then click **Submit**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deactivate (remove) a user

Deactivating a user removes their login access but preserves all their historical candidates and references.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the user">
    Go to **Settings → Users**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deactivate">
    Click the **deactivate** (or toggle) control next to the user. Confirm when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The user can no longer log in. Their past work remains visible and is still attributed to them in reports and activity logs.

## Manage sub-accounts

If your firm has **sub-accounts** enabled (visible under **Settings → Sub-accounts**), each sub-account has its own user list. To add a user to a sub-account, switch into that sub-account context and follow the same steps above.

<Note>
  A user can belong to more than one account within the same firm family. They will see a firm-switcher at login. A user cannot be added to two unrelated firm families.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The new user says they didn't receive their credentials email">
    Ask them to check their spam or junk folder. If the email still hasn't arrived after a few minutes, open the user's edit modal and click **Resend credentials** (if available), or deactivate and re-add the user to trigger a fresh invite.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't change the role for a specific user">
    The default root account's role is locked and cannot be changed. If the user is listed as the default account, contact Vitay support to transfer that designation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The Users tab is not visible">
    You need a root recruiter role to see user management. If you believe you should have admin access, ask your account's root recruiter to upgrade your role.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
