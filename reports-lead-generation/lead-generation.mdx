---
title: "Lead generation"
description: "Turn completed references into new business leads by capturing contact interest directly in the reference form."
---

When a reference completes a reference check for one of your candidates, they've already
demonstrated that they know the candidate's work — and they may be someone you'd want to work with
as a future candidate or client. Vitay's lead generation feature surfaces that interest
automatically: references can opt in to being contacted, and their details flow straight into your
leads pipeline.

<Note>
  Lead generation must be enabled on your account. Contact your admin to turn it on.
  You need a recruiter (or higher) role to view and export leads.
</Note>

## How it works

Your firm sets up one or more **lead types** — categories that describe the kind of contact you're
looking for (for example, "Candidate" or "Client"). Each lead type has:

- A **name** shown to references on the form.
- A **checkbox label** the reference ticks to opt in (e.g. "I'm open to hearing about new
  opportunities").
- An optional **thank-you message** shown after the reference completes the form.
- An optional **button** (label + link) if you want to direct leads to a landing page or
  application form.

When a reference finishes the reference check, the lead-gen card appears. The reference can tick
as many lead-type checkboxes as apply. When they do, Vitay records them as a lead under that type,
**along with a timestamped record of their consent**.

## Consent requirement

Vitay records two fields for every lead submission:

| Field | What it captures |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Consent Given At** | The exact date and time the reference ticked the checkbox and submitted the form |
| **Consent Text** | The exact wording of the checkbox label the reference agreed to at that moment |

This consent is **required** for a lead to be recorded. If the reference does not tick the
checkbox, no lead record is created. The consent timestamp and text are included in the
[leads CSV export](/reports-lead-generation/download-reports) so you have an auditable record.

## View your leads

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Leads">
    In the main navigation, click **Leads** (sometimes labeled **Lead Generation**).

    ![SCREENSHOT: the Leads section of the navigation and the leads list — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse or filter leads">
    The leads list shows each contact's name, email, lead type, the candidate they referenced,
    and the date they were captured. Use the filters to narrow by date range, recruiter, or scope
    (your leads vs. all leads vs. all sub-accounts).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View a lead's details">
    Click a lead to see their full contact information and the candidate they provided a reference
    for.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Export leads

To download your leads as a CSV file — including consent timestamps and text — see
[Download reports](/reports-lead-generation/download-reports) and choose the **Leads** or
**Custom leads** report type.

## Set up lead types

Lead types are configured at the firm level by an admin.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Click your firm name or avatar in the navigation, then go to **Settings** → **Lead
    Generation** (or **Lead types**).

    ![SCREENSHOT: the Lead types settings panel with an existing lead type — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a lead type">
    Click **Add lead type** (up to three custom types are supported). Fill in:
    - **Name** — internal label for this lead category.
    - **Checkbox label** — the opt-in text the reference sees on the form. Write it in plain
      language that clearly describes what they're consenting to, since this text is stored as
      the consent record.
    - **Thank-you text** (optional) — shown to the reference after they submit.
    - **Button text** and **Button link** (optional) — adds a call-to-action button on the
      thank-you screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure notifications">
    Each lead type has its own notification settings. Choose whether to alert the assigned
    recruiter, the account root user, or a custom email list whenever a new lead is captured
    under that type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The lead type becomes active immediately; it will appear on the reference form
    for any new reference check completed by a reference on your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A reference completed the form but I don't see a lead">
    Leads are only recorded when the reference actively ticks the consent checkbox. If they
    skipped it, no lead is created. You cannot add a lead on behalf of a reference.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The Leads section isn't visible in the navigation">
    Lead generation must be enabled on your account. Ask your account admin to turn it on in the
    firm settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I don't see a consent timestamp in the leads export">
    The **Consent Given At** and **Consent Text** columns are included automatically on all leads
    and custom-leads CSV exports. If they appear blank, the lead was captured before the consent
    feature was introduced — the contact did opt in, but the timestamp was not recorded at that
    time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I need to add more than three lead types">
    The platform currently supports up to three custom lead types per firm. Contact Vitay support
    if you need a different configuration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
