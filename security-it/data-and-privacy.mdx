Vitay handles personal data on behalf of the hiring firms that use it —
primarily the data that candidates and references submit during the reference
checking process. This article explains what data Vitay holds, how long it's
kept, and how it can be deleted.

<Note>
  For formal data processing agreements (DPAs), sub-processor lists, or
  questions about compliance with specific regulations (GDPR, SOC 2, etc.),
  contact [support@vitay.io](mailto:support@vitay.io). Vitay's legal and
  security teams handle those requests directly.
</Note>

## What data Vitay holds

Vitay stores the following categories of data on behalf of your firm:

- **Candidate data** — name, contact details, and the reference request(s) your
  team creates.
- **Reference data** — name, contact details, and the answers a reference
  submits in response to a reference request.
- **Recruiter and admin data** — account details (name, email, role) for
  members of your firm who use Vitay.
- **Activity data** — a log of actions taken in the platform (for example, when
  a reference request was sent or a response was received).

## Data residency

Vitay operates separate environments by region:

- **Australia / global** — `references.vitay.io`
- **Canada** — `references.vitay.ca`
- **United Kingdom** — `references.vitay.co.uk`

Data is stored in the region that corresponds to your firm's Vitay environment.
Reach out to [support@vitay.io](mailto:support@vitay.io) if you need to confirm
which region your firm is on.

## Data retention

Vitay retains candidate and reference data for as long as your firm's account is
active. Your firm controls when data is deleted (see below). Vitay does not
automatically expire individual reference records based on a fixed calendar
period — retention is governed by your firm's own policies and any contractual
terms with Vitay.

## Deleting data

### Deleting individual records

Recruiters and admins can delete individual candidates and references from within
the Vitay app. Deleted records are removed from the active application.

### Full firm data purge

When a firm's account is closed or on request, Vitay can purge all data
associated with that firm. A purge removes data across all related tables —
including answers, reference requests, candidates, recruiters, and the firm
record itself — in dependency order. The process is tracked and auditable
internally, and a rollback capability exists for a short window after initiation.

If you need to request a full data purge, contact
[support@vitay.io](mailto:support@vitay.io).

## Security measures

- All data is transmitted over **HTTPS/TLS**.
- Vitay authenticates recruiter sessions with signed tokens and supports
  **SAML SSO** for centralised access control — see
  [Single sign-on (SAML)](/security-it/sso-saml).
- Reference submission links are single-use and time-limited.
- Vitay monitors for errors and anomalies via application-level observability
  tooling.
