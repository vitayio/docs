description: "When and how Vitay reminds references to respond, and how to adjust it."
---

Vitay can automatically follow up with references (and candidates) who haven't responded yet. Understanding how reminders work helps you avoid over-contacting people and troubleshoot when a reference goes quiet.

<Note>
  Auto-reminder settings are controlled at the account level by a Vitay admin.
  As a recruiter you can send manual reminders and turn off reminders for
  individual candidates, but you cannot change the global reminder schedule.
</Note>

## How automatic reminders work

When a reference request or individual reference is sent, Vitay schedules a series of follow-up reminders automatically — provided your account has auto-reminders enabled.

- **Cadence:** reminders go out at a configurable interval (the default is every 24 hours). Your admin may have changed this.
- **Weekend skipping:** your admin can configure reminders to skip Saturday and Sunday, so references only receive nudges on weekdays.
- **Channels:** depending on your account settings, reminders are sent by email, by SMS, or both.
- **Limit:** there is a maximum number of reminders per request. Once that limit is reached, no further automatic reminders are sent.
- **Auto-stop:** if the reference completes the form or the request is cancelled, any pending reminders are deactivated immediately.

A reminder indicator on the candidate or reference record shows you how many reminders have been sent and when the next one is scheduled.

## Send a manual reminder

You can nudge a reference or a candidate at any time, outside the automatic schedule.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the candidate">
    Go to **Candidates** and select the candidate whose reference hasn't responded.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the reference or request">
    On the candidate page, locate the reference or reference request that needs a nudge. Open it to see its detail view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the reminder">
    Click **Send reminder**. Vitay sends the follow-up email (and SMS, if applicable) immediately.

    ![SCREENSHOT: the reference detail view with the "Send reminder" button — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The **Send reminder** button is greyed out or labelled **Reminders disabled**
  in two situations: reminders are turned off for this candidate, or the
  reminder rate limit has been reached (you sent one too recently). Wait a short
  period and try again.
</Note>

## Disable reminders for a candidate

If a candidate asks you to stop sending reminder emails, you can turn reminders off for their record without affecting other candidates.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the candidate">
    Go to **Candidates** and select the candidate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the candidate record">
    Click **Edit** and look for the reminders toggle. Turn it off, then save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When reminders are disabled for a candidate, the **Send reminder** button on their references and requests will show **Reminders disabled**.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The Send reminder button is greyed out">
    This usually means one of three things: the reference has already
    completed the form, reminders are disabled for this candidate, or the
    system's rate limit prevents sending another reminder so soon. Check the
    reference status first, then check the candidate's reminders setting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="A reference says they're receiving too many emails">
    Each account has a cap on total reminders per request. If a reference is
    still getting emails after many days, check with your Vitay admin to
    confirm the auto-reminder cadence and limit configured for your account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No reminders are going out at all">
    Your account's auto-reminders may be disabled. Ask a Vitay admin to check
    the auto-reminders setting. Individual manual reminders still work even when
    auto-reminders are off.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
