Before you can send a reference request, the candidate needs to exist in Vitay. Adding a candidate takes under a minute and only requires a name — you can fill in the rest later.

<Note>
  You need a recruiter (or higher) role to add candidates. If you don't see the
  option, ask your Vitay admin to check your permissions.
</Note>

## Add a candidate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Candidates list">
    In the left sidebar, click **Candidates**. This shows all active candidates
    in your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the add form">
    Click the **Add candidate** button (usually in the top-right corner of the
    Candidates page).

    ![SCREENSHOT: the Candidates page header with the "Add candidate" button highlighted — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the candidate's details">
    Complete the form fields:

    - **First name** and **Last name** — required.
    - **Preferred name** — optional; used in outgoing emails if provided.
    - **Email** — required by most firm configurations. Must be unique within your account.
    - **Mobile number** — optional; used for SMS reminders if your plan includes them.
    - **Internal ID** — your firm's own identifier (e.g., from your ATS). Click the refresh icon to auto-generate one if needed.
    - **Assigned user** — the recruiter responsible for this candidate. Defaults to you; admins and root users can assign someone else.
    - **Notes** — private notes visible only to recruiters, not to the candidate.
    - **Placement date** — optional; useful for tracking when the hire was made.

    Additional fields such as **Secondary ID**, **Job title**, and **Position applied for** appear only when your firm has those settings enabled.

    ![SCREENSHOT: the Add candidate form with key fields labelled — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit**. The candidate record is created and you're taken to their
    profile, where you can send a reference request right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

- The candidate appears in your **Candidates** list immediately.
- You can edit any field later by opening the candidate and clicking **Edit**.
- To begin collecting references, see [Send a reference request](/candidates-reference-requests/send-a-reference-request).

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I get an error saying the email already exists">
    Each email address can only be linked to one active candidate per account.
    Search for the email in the Candidates list — the record may already exist
    (possibly under a different name or marked inactive). If you need to
    reactivate an old record, contact your Vitay admin.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Some fields are missing from the form">
    Fields like Secondary ID, Job title, and Position applied for are controlled
    by your firm's settings. If you need them enabled, ask a Vitay admin to
    turn them on under account settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't assign the candidate to another recruiter">
    The **Assigned user** field is only editable by admins, root users, or when
    your firm has enabled candidate ownership transfers. Ask your admin if you
    need to reassign a candidate.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
