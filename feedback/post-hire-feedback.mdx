---
title: "Post-hire feedback"
description: "Ask an employer to share feedback on a placed candidate after they've started the role."
---

Post-hire feedback lets you check in on a candidate after you've placed them. You send a feedback
request to the employer (the hiring manager or company contact), and they answer a structured
question set. The completed feedback appears on the candidate's profile so you can track quality of
placement over time.

<Note>
  Post-hire feedback requires the **Employer feedback** feature to be enabled on your account.
  Contact your account admin if you don't see the option. You need a recruiter (or higher) role
  to send feedback requests.
</Note>

## Send a post-hire feedback request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the candidate">
    Go to **Candidates** and select the placed candidate you want to collect feedback on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a feedback request">
    Click **Request feedback**. A modal opens where you choose the feedback type.

    ![SCREENSHOT: the candidate profile page with the "Request feedback" button visible — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Employer type">
    In the **Type** field, select **Employer**. Fields for the employer's contact details appear
    below.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the employer's details">
    Fill in the employer's **first name**, **last name**, and **email address**. Add a phone number
    if you want Vitay to send an SMS reminder as well.

    ![SCREENSHOT: the feedback request modal with Employer selected and contact fields filled in — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a question set">
    Select the **question set** the employer will answer. If your firm only has one question set
    configured for feedback, it is pre-selected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule (optional)">
    If your firm has scheduled feedback enabled, you can pick a **scheduled date** to delay
    delivery. Leave this blank to send immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit**. Vitay emails the employer with a link to the feedback form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

- The employer receives an email with a link to complete the feedback form.
- Vitay automatically sends reminders if the employer hasn't responded — the number and timing of
  reminders follow your firm's reminder settings.
- When the employer submits their answers, you receive a notification email and the completed
  feedback appears under the candidate's **Feedback** tab.
- A score is calculated from any rating or matrix questions in the question set.

## View completed feedback

Open the candidate's profile and click the **Feedback** tab to see all completed and pending
feedback requests for that candidate.

![SCREENSHOT: the candidate's Feedback tab showing a completed employer feedback with score — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I don't see the Employer option in the feedback type selector">
    The Employer feedback type is controlled by a feature flag on your account. Ask your account
    admin to enable **Employer feedbacks** in the firm settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The employer says they didn't get the email">
    Check that the email address you entered is correct on the candidate's Feedback tab. If it
    looks right, ask the employer to check their spam folder. You can resend the request from the
    Feedback tab by finding the pending request and clicking **Resend**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The feedback request was sent to the wrong email">
    Open the candidate's Feedback tab, locate the pending request, and use the edit option to
    correct the employer's email address, then resend.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
