---
title: "Candidate feedback"
description: "Collect feedback directly from a candidate, or from an employer or recruiter, using Vitay's feedback request flow."
---

Vitay's feedback feature lets you send a structured questionnaire to a candidate, an employer, or
a recruiter. The recipient fills in the form online and the results appear on the candidate's
profile. You can use this to gather candidate experience data, placement check-ins, or internal
recruiter notes — all powered by the same question-set system used for references.

<Note>
  Which feedback types are available depends on your firm's settings. Candidate feedback requires
  **Candidate feedback** to be enabled; employer and recruiter variants are toggled separately.
  Contact your account admin if a type is missing from the selector. You need a recruiter (or
  higher) role to send feedback requests.
</Note>

## Feedback types

| Type | Who fills it in | Common use |
|------|-----------------|------------|
| **Candidate** | The candidate themselves | Candidate experience surveys, placement satisfaction |
| **Employer** | A hiring manager or company contact | Post-hire check-ins, client satisfaction |
| **Recruiter** | An internal recruiter | Internal quality notes (if enabled) |

## Send a feedback request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the candidate">
    Go to **Candidates** and select the candidate you want to collect feedback on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the feedback request form">
    Click **Request feedback** on the candidate's profile page.

    ![SCREENSHOT: the candidate profile page with the "Request feedback" button — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the feedback type">
    In the **Type** selector, pick **Candidate**, **Employer**, or **Recruiter**. The form adjusts
    based on your selection.

    - **Candidate** — Vitay emails the candidate directly using the email address already on their
      profile.
    - **Employer** — Enter the employer's first name, last name, and email. Add a phone number if
      you also want SMS reminders sent.
    - **Recruiter** — Vitay sends the form to the assigned recruiter (confirmation message shown).

    ![SCREENSHOT: the feedback request modal with the Type selector and question-set dropdown — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a question set">
    Select the **question set** from the dropdown. Only question sets configured for feedback are
    shown. If there is only one available, it is pre-selected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes (optional)">
    If you want to include a message the recipient will see on the form, add it in the **Notes**
    field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule (optional)">
    Pick a **scheduled date** to delay delivery, or leave it blank to send right away. Scheduled
    delivery requires the feature to be enabled on your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit**. Vitay sends the request by email (and SMS if a phone number was provided and
    text messages are enabled for your firm).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens after you send

- The recipient gets an email with a unique link to the feedback form.
- Vitay sends automatic reminders if the recipient hasn't responded, following your firm's reminder
  schedule.
- When the form is completed, you receive a notification and the feedback appears on the
  candidate's **Feedback** tab.
- Any rating or matrix questions generate a score you can see at a glance.

## View and export feedback

Open the candidate's **Feedback** tab to see all sent, pending, and completed feedback requests.
To export feedback answers in bulk, see [Download reports](/reports-lead-generation/download-reports).

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A feedback type is missing from the selector">
    Each type — Candidate, Employer, and Recruiter — is controlled by a separate feature flag in
    your firm's settings. Ask your account admin to enable the type you need.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The candidate didn't receive the feedback email">
    Check that the email address on the candidate's profile is correct. Ask the candidate to check
    their spam folder. You can resend from the candidate's Feedback tab.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I need to change the email address for an employer feedback">
    Open the candidate's Feedback tab, find the pending feedback request, and edit the employer's
    contact details before resending.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
