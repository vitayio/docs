Vitay integrates with popular applicant tracking systems (ATS) so your team can trigger reference checks without leaving the ATS and push completed reference reports back automatically.

<Note>
  Setting up an integration requires admin access in Vitay. The exact steps vary by ATS — see the article for your platform below.
</Note>


![The Integrations gallery in Configuration](/images/integrations.png)

## What integrations do

Once an integration is connected, your recruiters can:

- **Import candidates** from your ATS into Vitay with a single click.
- **Send reference requests** directly from the ATS candidate record.
- **Push completed reference PDFs** back into the candidate's ATS profile automatically.

Vitay stores the connection credentials securely and refreshes access tokens in the background, so day-to-day use requires no re-authentication.
