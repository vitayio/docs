The Google integration lets individual Vitay users connect their Google account and import their Google Contacts into the Vitay network. It is a per-user connection — each recruiter who wants to use it authorizes their own Google account separately.

<Note>
  This integration is linked at the **user** level, not the firm level. Each recruiter connects their own Google account. You will be redirected to Google to grant Vitay read-only access to your contacts.
</Note>

## What the integration does

- **Import Google Contacts** — reads your Google Contacts (name and email) and creates corresponding user records in the Vitay network, so people you already know can be found and invited more easily.
- **Read-only access** — Vitay only requests read access to your contacts and basic profile information. It does not write to Google or access your Gmail.

<Note>
  The Google Contacts import creates network entries for contacts who are not already in Vitay. It does not create candidates or send reference requests automatically.
</Note>

## Connect your Google account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your account settings">
    In Vitay, navigate to your profile or network settings where the Google connection option appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize with Google">
    Click the **Connect Google** button (or equivalent). You will be redirected to Google's OAuth consent screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant access">
    Sign in to your Google account and allow Vitay read-only access to your contacts and profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync contacts">
    After authorizing, Vitay fetches your contacts and adds any new ones to the network.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Disconnect Google

To remove the Google connection, contact [support@vitay.io](mailto:support@vitay.io) or revoke Vitay's access directly in your [Google account permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My contacts didn't import">
    Make sure you completed the Google OAuth flow and granted the contacts permission. If authorization was declined or interrupted, reconnect your account and try again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Some contacts are missing">
    Vitay imports contacts that have both a name and an email address. Contacts without an email are skipped.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
