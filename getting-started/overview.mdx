description: "What Vitay does and how to find your way around as a recruiter or admin."

Vitay automates your candidate reference-checking process. Instead of chasing references by phone, you
add a candidate, send a reference request, and Vitay handles the emails, reminders, and response
collection — so you can focus on evaluating results, not logistics.

This help center is written for **recruiters and admins** who have a Vitay login. If you received a
reference request email and need help completing it, you are in the wrong place — contact the recruiter
who sent you the request; their email address is in the request email.

## What you can do in Vitay

- **Add candidates and send reference requests** — invite candidates to name their references, then
  let Vitay collect and compile the responses automatically.
- **Review completed references** — read, score, and download reference reports from your dashboard.
- **Run feedback surveys** — collect post-hire and candidate-experience feedback.
- **Generate leads** — turn reference respondents into a talent pipeline.
- **Configure your account** — manage users, branding, question sets, and integrations.
