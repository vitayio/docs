---

All recruiters and admins sign in at [app.vitay.io](https://app.vitay.io). You need the email address
and password that were sent to you when your account was created.

## Sign in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to app.vitay.io">
    Open [app.vitay.io](https://app.vitay.io) in your browser. You will see the Vitay login page.

    ![SCREENSHOT: the Vitay login page showing email and password fields — placeholder](/images/placeholder.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and password">
    Type your email address and password, then click **Log in**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete MFA if prompted">
    If multi-factor authentication is enabled on your account, you will be redirected to a
    verification screen. Enter the code from your authenticator app or SMS, then click **Verify**.
    See [Multi-factor authentication & login options](/getting-started/mfa-and-login-options) for setup details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reset your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Forgot password'">
    On the login page, click the **Forgot password** link below the password field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Type your Vitay email address and submit the form. Vitay sends you a password-reset email with
    a link.

    <Note>
      Password reset emails are rate-limited. If you request more than two resets within a
      three-hour window, you will need to wait before requesting another one.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the link and set a new password">
    Click the link in the reset email and choose a new password. Passwords must be at least
    8 characters and include an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, a number, and a special
    character.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## SSO users

If your organization uses single sign-on (SAML/SSO), you may be redirected automatically when you
enter your email on the login page. You will complete authentication through your identity provider
rather than entering a Vitay password. For setup and troubleshooting, see
[SSO / SAML](/security-it/sso-saml).

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't remember my password">
    Use the **Forgot password** link on the login page. If you don't receive the reset email
    within a few minutes, check your spam folder. The email comes from Vitay's notification address.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My account is locked">
    Vitay locks accounts after 10 consecutive failed login attempts. Contact your account admin to
    unlock your account, or reach out to Vitay support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm being asked to verify my identity but I don't have the app">
    If you've lost access to your MFA device, contact your account admin. They can disable MFA on
    your profile so you can log in and re-enroll.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I see a 'password reset rate limit' message">
    You can request up to two password resets within any three-hour period. Wait until the window
    expires, then try again.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
