---
title: "User roles explained"
description: "What recruiters, supervisors, and admins can each do in Vitay."
---

Vitay uses roles to control what each person can see and do. When a root recruiter adds a new
user to your account, they choose the right role for that person.

<Note>
  Only the root recruiter (account owner) can invite and manage other users. If you need your role
  changed, ask your root recruiter or contact Vitay support.
</Note>

## Role overview

| Role | Who it's for | What they can do |
|------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Recruiter** | Individual team members | Add candidates and send reference requests scoped to their own work |
| **Supervisor** | Team leads or managers | View candidates and references across the whole account, not just their own |
| **Root Recruiter** | Account owner / admin | Full access: invite users, change settings, view all candidates and references, manage integrations |

<Note>
  The **admin** role is reserved for internal Vitay staff and is not assigned to customer accounts.
</Note>

## Recruiter

A recruiter is the standard role for day-to-day reference checking. Recruiters can:

- Add candidates and send reference requests
- View and download references they personally sent
- Update their own profile and notification preferences

By default, a recruiter sees only the candidates and references they added themselves. If your root
recruiter has enabled the **All toggle** setting for your account, recruiters may also see work
belonging to their colleagues — this is a firm-wide setting your root recruiter can configure.

## Supervisor

A supervisor is a recruiter who has been granted an extra permission by the root recruiter. Supervisors
can:

- Do everything a recruiter can
- View all candidates and references across the account, regardless of who added them

The supervisor role is additive — a user must already be a recruiter before the root recruiter can
grant them supervisor access.

## Root Recruiter

The root recruiter is the account owner and has full control over the account. Root recruiters can:

- Do everything a recruiter can
- View all candidates and references across the account
- Invite, edit, and deactivate other users
- Assign or revoke the supervisor role for other users
- Configure account settings (question sets, branding, email settings, notifications)
- Manage integrations (Bullhorn, JobAdder, and others)
- Access analytics across all sub-accounts (if sub-accounts are enabled)

Each Vitay account has at least one root recruiter. If your organization uses sub-accounts, each
sub-account has its own root recruiter who manages that account's users and settings independently.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't see another recruiter's candidates">
    By default, recruiters only see their own candidates. Ask your root recruiter to enable the
    **All toggle** setting, or ask them to grant you the supervisor role.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I don't see the Settings or Users menu">
    Only root recruiters can access account settings and user management. If you need those
    capabilities, ask your root recruiter to update your role.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
