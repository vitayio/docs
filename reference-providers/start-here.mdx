<Note>
  **Are you a recruiter or hiring manager using Vitay?** This section is for people who were asked to *give* a reference — it won't cover the recruiter app. Head to the [recruiter docs](/candidates-reference-requests/overview) instead.
</Note>

Welcome, and thank you for taking the time to complete a reference. Someone listed you as a reference for a job opportunity, and the company's recruiting team uses Vitay to collect that information securely online.

You don't need a Vitay account. Everything you need is in the reference request email you received — including a link to the form and the name of the recruiter who sent the request.

**If something isn't working**, the quickest path forward is to reply to your request email or contact the recruiter directly. Their name and email are in the email Vitay sent you.
