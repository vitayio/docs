---
title: "My reference isn't receiving the email"
description: "Steps to take if someone you listed as a reference hasn't received the Vitay request email."
---

If you provided someone's contact details as a reference and they're telling you they never got the email, don't worry — this is usually a quick fix.

Work through the steps below before asking the recruiter to resend, so you can give them the most useful information.

## Steps to check

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask them to check spam or junk">
    Vitay's emails sometimes land in spam, especially if the recipient's company has aggressive email filters. Ask your reference to check their **Spam**, **Junk**, or **Promotions** folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the email address is correct">
    Double-check the address you provided. A single typo (like `.con` instead of `.com`) means the email went somewhere else entirely. If you think the address might be wrong, see [Updating a reference's email address](/reference-providers/change-reference-email).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check whether their work network is blocking it">
    Some corporate networks block emails that contain external links. Ask your reference to check on their personal email or phone if the work inbox is the problem.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask the recruiter to resend">
    If the email still hasn't arrived after checking the above, reply to your original Vitay request email and ask the recruiter to resend the reference request. Let them know you've already checked spam and the address looks correct — that helps them troubleshoot faster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Only the recruiter can resend the request email. There is no way to trigger a resend from the candidate side.
</Note>
