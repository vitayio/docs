---
title: "I don't recognize this reference request"
description: "What to do if you received a Vitay reference request you weren't expecting or don't recognize."
---

Getting an unexpected reference request can feel alarming, but it's usually straightforward to explain: a job candidate listed you as one of their references and gave your contact details to their recruiter. You may not have been told in advance.

You are not obligated to complete the reference, and there's nothing you need to do to "refuse" formally — you can simply not fill it in.

## Your options

**Not sure if you know the candidate?**

The request email names the candidate you're being asked to reference. If you recognize the name, the request is legitimate. If you genuinely don't know the person, use one of the options below.

**Option 1 — Decline using the form**

Open the reference link in the email. Most Vitay reference forms have a **Decline** option that lets you indicate you don't wish to (or are unable to) complete the reference. This notifies the recruiter automatically.

**Option 2 — Contact the recruiter**

Reply to the request email or email the recruiter directly (their name and contact are in the email). Let them know you don't recognize the candidate or didn't agree to be listed. They'll follow up with the candidate to sort it out.

**Option 3 — Do nothing**

If you don't respond, the recruiter will eventually follow up. There's no penalty for not completing a form you didn't agree to.

<Note>
  If you believe someone used your email address fraudulently, reply to the request email and tell the recruiter immediately. They can remove your details and investigate.
</Note>
