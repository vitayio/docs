---
title: "Changing or updating a reference's email"
description: "How to correct or update the email address for a reference you provided."
---

If you gave the wrong email address for one of your references — or if their email has changed — there's no self-serve way to correct it from the reference form. Email addresses are managed by the recruiter in Vitay.

The good news is that the recruiter can update the address and resend the request in seconds.

## What to do

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the recruiter's contact details">
    Open the original reference request email you received from Vitay. The recruiter's name appears in the message, and replying to that email will reach them directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the correct email address">
    Tell the recruiter which reference needs updating and provide the correct email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The recruiter updates and resends">
    Once they make the change, Vitay will send a fresh request to the corrected address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If the reference told you they never got the email, it may be a delivery issue rather than a wrong address — see [A reference I listed isn't getting the email](/reference-providers/reference-not-receiving-email) for steps to check first.
</Note>
