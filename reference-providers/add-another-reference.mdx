---
title: "Adding or providing a new reference"
description: "How to add another reference after you've already submitted your reference form."
---

Thank you for completing your reference. If the recruiter needs you to provide details for an additional person — or if you realize you left someone out — there's no self-serve way to add more references from your end.

The recruiter manages the reference list on their side. All you need to do is get in touch with them.

## What to do

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the recruiter's contact details">
    Open the original reference request email you received from Vitay. The recruiter's name is mentioned in the email, and you can reply directly to reach them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let them know">
    Tell the recruiter that you'd like to add another reference (or that they should add one). Include the person's name and email address if you have it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The recruiter adds the reference">
    Once the recruiter updates the list, Vitay will send the new reference their own request email automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you can't find the original email, check your spam or junk folder — it was sent from a Vitay-powered address on behalf of the recruiter.
</Note>
